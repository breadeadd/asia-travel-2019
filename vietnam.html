HO CHI MINH CITY

3/7-6/7 - DAY 1

Travelling to Ho Chi Minh in Vietnam, flying at 11:50 pm, arriving at 1 pm in Vietnam. Having chocolate at Marou and going to a local market.

4/7 - DAY 2

The first full day. We visited temples and going to Bảo tàng Chứng tích Chiến Tranh War Remnants museum looking at old war items.

5/7 - DAY 3

Củ Chi Tunnels, learning about the tunnels which that were used during the Vietnam war.

HỘI AN

6/7-9/7 - DAY 4

Leaving Ho Chi Minh City to go to Hội An continuing our adventures in Vietnam. Following our arrival, we roamed the streets.

7/7 - DAY 5

We used bicycles to travel from temple to temple, sightseeing and boat rides on the river to cool off. Before seeing all the lanterns light up at night.

8/7- DAY 6

Tour to Mỹ Sơn, Marble Mountains and the giant Lady Buddha statue, finishing the day feasting on seafood next to the beach.

9/7- DAY 7

A day out to the pottery village. We had the chance to try out pottery making. Finishing the day by having a fancy dinner and Gong cha for Calvin's birthday.
